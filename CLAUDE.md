# CLAUDE — Portfolio-05 Design Constitution

## Core Principles
- **Clarity Before Creativity** — never sacrifice usability for aesthetics
- **Business Before Decoration** — every element must serve a business goal
- **One Purpose Per Section** — focused sections outperform multi-purpose ones
- **Purposeful Motion** — animations must guide attention, not decorate
- **AI Supports Human Judgment** — AI accelerates execution; strategy stays human-led

## Portfolio Mission
Demonstrate strategic thinking and business value through UI/UX design, not just visual aesthetics. The portfolio is a product experience, not a résumé.

## Target Audience
Startup founders, product managers, design agencies, recruiters.

## 30-Second Rule
A visitor must understand in 30 seconds:
1. What I do
2. How I solve business problems
3. How I combine design thinking with AI

## Content Architecture
Every section follows: Label → Narrative Hook → Supporting Idea → CTA

## Tone
Professional, direct, action-oriented, authoritative. Never use résumé language.
