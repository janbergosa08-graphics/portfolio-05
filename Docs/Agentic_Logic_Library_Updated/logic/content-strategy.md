# Narrative Content Protocol

## The "Conversion Story" Structure
For every project, section, or component:
1. **Pinpoint Pain:** Define the friction point in a single, emotionally resonant sentence.
2. **Map Process:** Chart three steps: Explore Research -> Architect Ideation -> Deliver Impact.
3. **Quantify Proof:** Anchor every result in business reality (e.g., Increase Retention, Slash Delivery Time).

## Tone of Voice
- **Dynamic Expert:** Maintain a professional, conversational tone (The "Coffee Chat" test).
- **Evocative:** Use sensory-rich verbs. Focus on the result, not the tool.
- **Problem-Aware:** Position the solution as a familiar, expert fix for their known issues.
